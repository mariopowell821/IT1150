The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxuary yurts are permanet structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed wih canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opend.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is funished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sin with hot and cold runnig water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfort walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bringa flashlight and sense of adventure!
